At River Tech Middle School, we’ve reimagined education to capture the vibrant energy and transformative potential of the middle school years. Here, education goes beyond traditional boundaries, resonating deeply with the adventurous spirit of adolescence. Our smaller class sizes allow for deep, meaningful connections, fostering an environment where each student is seen, valued, and empowered to grow at their own pace.

We combine the latest in technology with an expansive arts program, creating a rich tapestry of creative and critical thinking opportunities. This innovative approach is grounded in our Christian values and brought to life by our passionate educators, each an expert in their field, guiding students on a journey of self-discovery and intellectual courage.

At River Tech Middle School, learning is an adventure that extends well beyond the classroom walls. Our world is rich with interactive learning opportunities and field trips, inspiring students to explore new horizons and embrace personal growth.

We warmly invite you to discover the unique, empowering, and dynamic world of River Tech Middle School at our next open house event.
