River Tech Middle School is designed for the energy and curiosity of the middle school years. Our smaller class sizes create real, meaningful connections, and every student grows at their own pace.

We pair current technology with a broad arts program, giving students space for both creative and critical thinking. Our Christian values ground everything we do, and our educators, each an expert in their field, walk alongside students as they discover who they are and what they can do.

Learning at River Tech Middle School reaches well beyond the classroom. Field trips and hands-on experiences keep students exploring, growing, and engaged with the world around them.
