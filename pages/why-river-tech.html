Why Choose River Tech?

At River Tech each child learns at their own pace.

River Tech is designed for artistic & tech-savvy kids who love music and technology. Along with core subjects (like Math, Language Arts, Social Science, and Science), students learn technology (coding, robotics, film making, and digital arts) and performing arts (music, band, choir, acting, dance, and musical theater).

Grounded in enduring Christian values and a family-centered culture, students receive the social & academic education necessary to succeed in life. With class sizes around 15 students, roughly half that of public schools, our teachers are able to spend more time with each student. With competency-based learning, each student learns at their own pace, while also having the benefits of a positive social environment.

A student begins at a certain level of competency and proceeds to the next level when he has mastered the level he is at. This competency based method replaces artificial "grades" and allows each student to learn at their own pace.

If a student excels at a subject, he can move faster, and if he finds a subject harder he can move slower. A child can be at different levels in different tracks.
