Sports & Fitness

We're excited to introduce a new branch at River Tech! Along with Performing Arts and Technology, we are building an Athletics/Physical Fitness program. The goal is to inspire students to maintain an active, healthy lifestyle. We want students to learn how to take charge of their bodies and train for a long, healthy life.

We need to be ready for what the world throws at us and what God may ask of us. That means being prepared physically as well as spiritually. Physical activity, like fasting, is a way we “afflict our souls” to bring our flesh into submission and draw closer to the Lord.

“Or do you not know that your body is a temple of the Holy Spirit within you, whom you have from God? You are not your own, for you were bought with a price. So glorify God in your body.”

– 1 Corinthians 6:19-20 ESV

The principle is clear: our bodies belong to the Lord, and they should reflect His glory just as the Tabernacle and Temple did. That means maintaining our bodies well, which includes physical fitness and mental toughness.

Join us as we take a truly holistic approach to education, raising the next generation to be both creative and resilient in the Lord.

Sincerely,
Jerome Long
