Interactive Curriculum Explorer

Tabbed interface for exploring different learning tracks. Click each tab to see details.

Core Academics

Math, Language Arts, Social Science, and Science form the academic foundation. Using competency-based progression, students advance when they've mastered the material — not on an arbitrary calendar. This means a 4th grader doing 6th-grade math can keep going, while another student needing extra time in reading gets it without stigma.

Technology Track

Coding (Scratch → Python → C# with Unity), Robotics (Vex → Arduino), 3D Design (Blender + 3D Printing), filmmaking, digital arts, videography, and sound engineering. Students build real projects — games, robots, animations, and films — while developing problem-solving and engineering skills.

Performing Arts Track

Every student learns drums, bass, ukulele, guitar, keyboard, and vocals. Band, choir, dance, acting, and musical theater round out the program. River Tech produces full musicals each year — Frozen, Journey to Bethlehem, The Greatest Showman, Annie, and The Jungle Book are among the productions so far.

Life Skills

Relationships, finances, health, cooking, and practical life management. River Tech teaches students not just academics, but how to navigate the real world with wisdom, confidence, and character. These are the lessons that last a lifetime.
