High School

River Tech High School in Post Falls, Idaho brings together technology, performing arts, and strong Christian ethics to prepare students in grades 9–12 for a successful life after graduation.

Important: River Tech High only accepts new students entering as Freshmen or Sophomores (ages 15 and under). This ensures all graduates experience our complete four-year program.

At River Tech High, we prepare students to shape their own futures with clarity and conviction. Smaller class sizes mean deeper connections and personalized learning, so each student can excel at their own pace.

Technology and performing arts work side by side here, creating an environment built for innovation and creativity. Our Christian values guide everything we do, helping us develop students who are strong academically and grounded in character.

At River Tech High School, learning goes far beyond the classroom. Interactive experiences and field trips keep students engaged, exploring, and growing in every area of life.
