Volunteer at River Tech

🤝 River Tech Volunteer · August 2026

Share your gifts. Shape the next generation.

This page is still under development. Check back soon.

1. Why volunteer?

  • Maintain our ~1:15 teacher–student ratio and keep classes small
  • Enrich lessons with real-world expertise
  • Model servant leadership and Christian values
  • Keep tuition affordable for every family
  • Join a community of creative, tech-savvy innovators

3. Who can apply?

Adults 18+ who meet both criteria:

A. Education / expertise (one or more):

  • College degree (any discipline)
  • Relevant industry certification
  • Military service with leadership or technical specialization
  • Proven entrepreneurial or professional experience

B. Direct work with children or youth:

  • Classroom teaching or homeschool co-op instruction
  • Church programs / Sunday school
  • Tutoring or mentoring
  • Camps, scouts, Civil Air Patrol, coaching, etc.

All volunteers must pass an Idaho background check. River Tech covers the fee.

4. Commitment & Reliability

By accepting a volunteer role, you agree to be present every week during your assigned slot. Notify learn@rivertech.me at least 24 hours in advance if you must be absent.

5. The process

  1. Submit the Volunteer Application (~10 minutes).
  2. If shortlisted, schedule an interview time.
  3. Complete the background-check consent.
  4. Onboarding: Early January.
  5. Start serving students!

6. Perks & support

  • Priority eligibility for River Tech tuition scholarships
  • Service-hour certificates and résumé/college-app letters
  • Annual “Volunteer Appreciation Brunch” & community networking
  • Discounts and priority seating at student performances/events
  • First dibs on piloting new programs and events
