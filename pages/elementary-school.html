River Tech Elementary School is built on heart and purpose. Our small class sizes mean real connections and personal attention for every child.

Every child’s learning path is different, and we celebrate that. We blend hands-on technology with the arts in an atmosphere that keeps young learners excited to come to school. All of it is grounded in our Christian values, because we’re here to nurture young minds, hearts, and spirits.

Our educators are experts in their fields and deeply committed to sparking a lifelong love of learning. They guide and encourage every child, making each school day one of joy and discovery.

River Tech Elementary is a community where every student is seen, valued, and encouraged to reach their full potential. Come experience it for yourself at our next open house.
