Scholarship Application

River Tech School of Performing Arts & Technology

Welcome. This application is for families seeking need-based tuition assistance for the 2026–27 school year. It takes about five minutes.

Idaho’s Parental Choice Tax Credit covers up to $5,000 per child ($7,500 for special-needs students) and is the starting point for every family. River Tech’s own scholarship program is smaller and more selective than in past years, with priority given to current students and to families who are actively volunteering with the school. Learn about the tax credit →

All applications are held on file and reviewed together in late July, once enrollment has taken shape. You’ll receive an email then with the decision and next steps; additional documentation may be requested if your application advances. A non-refundable $20 processing fee is collected at submit. Questions? learn@rivertech.me.

Who are you applying for?

Add each child you’re requesting assistance for. You can include up to 6.

How much assistance would help?

After the tax credit and your own planning, what additional support would bridge the gap to affording enrollment?

Briefly describe any financial hardship your family is experiencing. Share only what you’re comfortable with — no proof of income is required at this stage.
