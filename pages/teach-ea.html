Now Hiring · Combined Role · 2026–27

Teacher + Executive Assistant

One person, two callings — the classroom and the founder’s office.

This is our rarest opening, and it exists for a particular kind of person: someone who can form students in the classroom and keep a growing school running behind the scenes. We serve about 130 students in grades 1–12 — and this role touches all of it.

Prefer one calling at a time? See the full-time teacher, part-time teaching, and Executive Assistant roles on their own.

🎓

You Teach

Real classes, real students — the subjects you love, in classes of about fifteen, on a Christian foundation. Everything in the full-time teacher posting applies here.

🧠

You Run the Office

AI agents, writing, inbox, calendar — the craft of the Executive Assistant role, giving the founder his hours back.

🗓️

One Week, Two Rhythms

Teaching hours in the classroom; office hours around them. The exact blend is shaped person-by-person — with you, not for you.

🚀

Grow With Us

A young school, still being built. The person who holds both the classroom and the office grows with everything we become.

Who We’re Looking For

Everything the teaching role asks — a bachelor’s degree, real experience teaching a class of children, and a grounding in Christian faith — plus the operator’s gifts:

  • A teacher’s heart with an operator’s hands — you love forming students, and you also love it when things run.
  • AI-fluent — you direct AI agents to do real work, and check that work like a professional.
  • A strong writer — emails, parent notes, and documents that sound right the first time.
  • Calm, discreet, fast, and precise — trusted with confidential work in a busy school.

Both postings describe you? Then this page is yours. Read the full-time teacher and Executive Assistant pages in full — this role is simply both, held by one person.

Compensation

Built person-by-person, because this role is. Your qualifications set the conversation — and the stronger they are, the stronger the offer. We’d rather meet you than quote you.

How to Apply

1

Read both postings — the teacher and the Executive Assistant — and our culture.

2

Apply once — through the full-time teacher application. Check the “teacher + Executive Assistant” box and tell us about your office and AI skills.

3

We shape it together — a conversation, a visit, and a blend of hours built around your gifts.

Can you see yourself here — in both rooms? If something in you leaned forward, we would love to hear from you.

Apply via the Full-Time Teacher Form
