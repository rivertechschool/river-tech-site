Field Trip Registration

Wednesday, April 29, 2026

For River Tech students only

Sign-up deadline: Sunday, April 26. There will be no school on April 29, so all students who attend on Wednesdays are signing up for one of these two field trips. Spots are limited. Drop-off is at the field trip location.
Which trip?

Pick the trip your student will be attending. Each trip has its own drop-off location and schedule.

Schedule

  • 9:45 AM: Drop-off at Christian Karate Coeur d'Alene
  • 10:00–11:00 AM: Karate workshop (self-defense, fitness, discipline, character from a Biblical perspective)
  • 11:15 AM: School bus to CDA City Park
  • 11:30 AM–2:00 PM: Lunch, Fort Sherman playground, beach time
  • 2:00 PM: Pickup at Rotary Bandshell (CDA City Park)

What to bring

  • Backpack, comfortable athletic clothes & shoes
  • Large water bottle (or two)
  • Cold lunch and snacks
  • Extra t-shirt (recommended)
  • Sunscreen (weather dependent)
  • Optional: swimsuit + towel
  • No electronic devices

Homeschool families: parents should plan to attend with their student. Responsible students age 11+ may request drop-off permission below.

  • 2:00 PM: Pickup at Rotary Bandshell (CDA City Park)

What to bring

  • Sunscreen (if sunny)
