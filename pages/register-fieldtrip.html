Silverwood Field Trip

Monday, June 1, 2026

Open to River Tech full-time and homeschool students and their families

Sign-up & payment deadline: Thursday, May 28. After May 28 you'd need to pay full park pricing directly to Silverwood.
📅Monday, June 1, 2026 — all day
🎢Silverwood Theme Park
💵$35 per person · Free for Read 2 Ride students (at the gate)
📝Sign up & pay by Thursday, May 28
Show full trip details (schedule, what to bring, park rules)

Schedule

  • 8:50 AM — Student drop-off at River Tech / The Heart (full-time students riding the bus)
  • 9:00 AM — School bus departs from River Tech
  • 9:45 AM — School bus arrives at Silverwood / security check
  • 10:00 AM — Everyone meets at the ticket windows by the bathrooms to split into groups and receive tickets. Mary will hand out tickets here, both Read 2 Ride and paid.
  • 10:30 AM — Through the ticket gate, wait near the park entrance
  • 11:00 AM — Park opens. Full-time students stay with their assigned chaperone all day. Group switches require agreement of both chaperones and communication with Mary/Dan.
  • 4:30 PM — Begin heading back to the bus if riding back. Full-time students arriving in a private vehicle may stay longer with Mary/Dan's knowledge.
  • 5:00 PM — School bus departs Silverwood
  • 5:45 PM — School bus arrives back at River Tech for pick-up
  • 6:00 PM — Silverwood closes

What to bring

  • Cold lunch and dinner — or money to purchase food in the park
  • Snacks — or money for snacks
  • Large water bottle filled with cold water (mandatory for every student)
  • Hydration drinks (recommended)
  • Sunscreen and a sun hat (recommended)
  • Light, weather-appropriate clothes
  • Extra clothes and a small towel (recommended — many students will get wet on certain rides)
  • Phone to contact parents (optional)

Park access & rules

  • No tablets, laptops, or other devices.
  • No swimsuits — we are not going to Boulder Beach. Swimsuits are not allowed in the roller coaster park.
  • Boulder Beach is closed for this trip.

Read 2 Ride (free ticket)

Students who completed the Read 2 Ride Program (and already emailed their reading log to Mary) get a free ticket at the gate. If your child's log was left in the old building after the fire, don't worry — it's all taken care of.

Questions?

Text Mary at 425-444-2271. Please allow 24–48 hours for a reply.
