Enrollment

Availability

Want to enroll? Spots are open for next school year, starting August 25th, 2026.

With our growing team of teachers, we're ready to welcome additional students to River Tech starting August 25th, 2026. Apply today to secure your spot.

We offer two programs:

River Tech School (Full-Time)
The complete River Tech experience: four to five days of integrated learning in performing arts, technology, and core academics — designed to shape confident, purposeful students grounded in Christian values and prepared for life.

River Tech À La Carte (Home School)
Choose specific River Tech classes to enrich your homeschool. Access our performing arts and technology programs in a community of learners while maintaining your family's educational rhythm.

Tuition

Fall, 2026

Payment Plans

Tuition can be paid monthly, quarterly, or annually.

Quarterly is our standard plan and offers the best rate.

Monthly distributes tuition over twelve months (July–June).

Annual allows families to pay once at the start of the school year.

Scholarships & Tax Credit Programs

Financial assistance is available through the Idaho Parental Choice Tax Credit. Families using the tax credit may apply it toward upcoming quarters.

What Tuition Covers

Tuition includes all textbooks, classroom materials, and access to technology and performing arts equipment. Students bring their own laptop or tablet for schoolwork. (See Technology Requirements.)

Questions or Assistance Needed?
Please don't hesitate to reach out to us at learn@rivertech.me for any queries or further information.

Early Registration

Priority Consideration.

Take an active step towards securing a spot for your child by completing early registration. While it doesn't guarantee placement, it prioritizes your application, enhancing your child's chances of joining River Tech.

Please note that there are two registration forms available:

(A) Enroll in our 4- or 5-day school program starting August 25th.
(B) Enroll in our 1-4 day programs starting August 28th.

Application Fee

Invest in Potential.

Alongside early registration, submit the application fee. This fee helps us invest in the potential of each student and facilitates a comprehensive review of applications. This registration fee is per household.

If your application is denied, you will receive a 40% refund.

Registration for fall 2026: please email learn@rivertech.me to begin the process.

Review Process

Await Results.

Upon enrollment, our dedicated team undertakes a detailed examination of your child's application. We assess both academic achievements and the degree to which each applicant embodies the values of the River Tech community.

After our comprehensive review, we will swiftly communicate the outcome to your family. An official notification will be sent, indicating whether your child's application has been accepted. We understand the importance of this moment and strive to provide a timely response to assist with your family's planning.

For the families who will be joining the River Tech community, we are thrilled to extend a warm welcome. To ensure a smooth transition and a successful start at River Tech, we will provide all essential information, including tuition details, school supplies, school policies, and more, to fully prepare you for the journey ahead.

Choosing River Tech is a step toward a comprehensive educational pathway from kindergarten through high school, enriched with college coaching. It's a decision that goes beyond academics, embracing a future where your children are empowered with the values, creativity, and technological tools to thrive in an ever-changing world.

We deeply appreciate the trust placed in us by all families during the application process. Whether joining the River Tech family now or considering us in the future, we value the educational aspirations and paths of all students.
