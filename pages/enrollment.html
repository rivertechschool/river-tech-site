Enrollment

Full-Time Registration Homeschool Registration

A friendly reminder: early enrollment closes June 5. Between now and June 5 you can enroll in any program at our early rate. After June 5, we move to limited enrollment and the registration fee increases — so if you’re leaning toward joining us, this is the easy window.

Availability

Spots are open for the 2026–27 school year, starting September 1st, 2026. With our growing team of teachers, we're ready to welcome more students. Apply today to secure your spot.

We offer two programs:

River Tech School (Full-Time)
The complete River Tech experience: four to five days of integrated learning in performing arts, technology, and core academics, designed to build confident students grounded in Christian values and prepared for life.

River Tech À La Carte (Home School)
Choose specific River Tech classes to enrich your homeschool. Access our performing arts and technology programs alongside a community of learners while keeping your family's educational rhythm.

Tuition

Fall, 2026

Payment Plans

Tuition can be paid monthly, quarterly, or annually.

Quarterly is our standard plan and offers the best rate.

Monthly distributes tuition over twelve months (July–June).

Annual allows families to pay once at the start of the school year.

Scholarships & Tax Credit Programs

Financial assistance is available through the Idaho Parental Choice Tax Credit. Families using the tax credit may apply it toward upcoming quarters.

What Tuition Covers

Tuition includes all textbooks, classroom materials, and access to technology and performing arts equipment. Students bring their own laptop or tablet for schoolwork. (See Technology Requirements.)

Questions?
Reach out anytime at learn@rivertech.me.

Early Registration

Priority Consideration.

Complete early registration to give your child's application priority. It doesn't guarantee placement, but it does move your family to the front of the line.

Two registration forms are available:

(A) Enroll in our 4- or 5-day school program starting September 1st.
(B) Enroll in our 1-4 day programs starting September 2nd.

Application Fee

Submit the application fee along with your early registration. This per-household fee supports the review process for each applicant.

If your application is denied, you will receive a 40% refund.

Registration for fall 2026: please email learn@rivertech.me to begin the process.

Review Process

Our team reviews each application, looking at academic background and how well each family aligns with the River Tech community and values.

Once the review is complete, we'll notify your family promptly. We know this matters, and we want to give you time to plan.

Accepted families receive everything they need for a smooth start: tuition details, school supplies, policies, and more.

River Tech offers a complete educational path from elementary through high school, with college coaching built in. Your children will grow in character, creativity, and real-world skills.

We appreciate every family that applies. Whether you join now or in the future, we value the trust you place in us.

Our Culture

River Tech is a Christian school built on biblical principles, with prayer, worship, and gratitude woven into daily life. We affirm God, family, community, personal responsibility, patriotism, merit, grit, and entrepreneurship — and we do not adopt the ideological frameworks of contemporary progressive culture, such as gender ideology, critical race theory, victim mentality, or cancel culture.
