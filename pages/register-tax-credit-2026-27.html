River Tech School of Performing Arts & Technology

This form is for families who received the Idaho Parental Choice Tax Credit and want to put it toward 2026–27 tuition. If you didn’t receive the credit, no action is needed.

Tell us your choice below and you’ll see your exact payment update live as you go. Please use this form rather than replying by email — it lets us set up your invoices correctly. Have a special situation? Email us anytime at learn@rivertech.me.
