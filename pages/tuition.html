Tuition

2026–27 School Year

River Tech offers flexible enrollment from 1-day à la carte to 5-day full-time. Families choose how many days per week their student attends, and tuition scales accordingly.

Full-Time (4–5 Days · 8:30–2:30), Start September 1st 2026:
Includes all core subjects (Math, English, Science, and Social Studies) plus Technology, Performing Arts, Sports, and Life Skills.

À La Carte (1–4 Days · 10:30–2:30), Start September 2nd 2026:
Supplemental enrichment for homeschool families who teach core subjects at home.

Tuition can be paid monthly, quarterly, or annually. Quarterly is our standard plan and offers the best rate.

Setup Fees

A one-time annual enrollment fee per household covering materials, setup, and preparation for the year.

A friendly reminder: early enrollment closes June 5. Between now and June 5 you can enroll in any program at our early rate. After June 5, we move to limited enrollment and the registration fee increases — so if you’re leaning toward joining us, this is the easy window.

Scholarships & Tax Credit Programs

Financial assistance is available through the Idaho Parental Choice Tax Credit. Families using the tax credit may apply it toward upcoming quarters. Additional scholarships are also available.

What Tuition Covers

Tuition includes all textbooks, classroom materials, and access to technology and performing arts equipment. Students bring their own laptop or tablet for schoolwork.
