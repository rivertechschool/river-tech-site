Performing Arts

Where passion meets creativity and every voice finds its melody.

The Performing Arts live at the very heart of River Tech's identity. Music, drama, and dance awaken creativity, discipline, and joy in students, shaping both their character and their confidence. Through the arts, they learn to tell stories that matter, to move hearts, and to bring beauty into a world that desperately needs it.

At River Tech, we see artistic expression not just as entertainment but as a calling — an opportunity to reflect the Creator's love of beauty and order through song, movement, and theater.

Our Arts Team

DH

Dan Hegelund

Arts Director

Dan Hegelund, principal and arts director at River Tech, is a professional vocal coach, choir director, and music director. He has thirty years of experience teaching and producing music and heads the River Tech Arts department. Expect concerts, musicals, studio recordings, music videos, choir trips, talent shows, music festivals, etc.

CR

Caitlin Relvas

Dance Director

Caitlin Relvas has a B.A. in English and Classical Civilizations. She has a minor in Dance and has been studying and teaching dance for most of her life. Caitlin loves to teach K-1 students problem-solving skills and responsibility and teach middle and high school English students the art of writing and an appreciation for story.

Musicals

At River Tech School of Performing Arts & Technology, our annual musicals are a celebration of talent, teamwork, and imagination. Every week, students dive into singing, dancing, and acting, while a behind-the-scenes crew brings the magic to life with props, sets, and lighting. Open to anyone willing to audition and commit, each musical challenges students to push their skills further, resulting in productions that are bigger, bolder, and more spectacular each year.

Instruments

Students can dive into the world of music by learning instruments like piano, guitar, and drums. Weekly lessons build strong foundational skills, while bands and our worship team give students the chance to perform, collaborate, and lead music in real settings. Whether jamming with friends, learning new pieces, or performing at assemblies, River Tech provides an inspiring environment for students to grow their musical talents and discover the joy of making music together.

Our Musical Performances

December 2025

Frozen

Frozen promises an enchanting experience, with breathtaking band performances and captivating dance routines that bring this cherished tale to life. Performances took place on December 10th and 11th at 6:00 PM.

December 2024

Journey to Bethlehem

"Journey to Bethlehem" was a remarkable production, taking our band performances and dance routines to new heights. We told the timeless story of Jesus' birth to two packed audiences. What a memorable way to celebrate Christmas.

April 2024

The Greatest Showman

"The Greatest Showman" was a vibrant showcase of how far we've come since our first musical. We featured an expanded repertoire of songs and dances, reflecting the substantial growth in our students' artistic talents. The band and dance troupe collaboratively reached new heights. Multiple soloists took the stage, each showcasing significantly enhanced vocal performances.

June 2023

The Lion King

River Tech's production of "The Lion King" set a new standard for our musical performances. This ambitious rendition featured 16 lapel microphones, two bands, advanced dance routines, and an extended script. A special mention to Daenerys, who, as Timba, captured the hearts of the audience with impeccable comedic timing.

December 2022

Annie

With "Annie," River Tech took a bold step forward in musical theater. We started incorporating more complex elements like dance routines, live band performances, and introduced lapel microphones for the first time.

June 2022

The Jungle Book — Our First Musical

Our inaugural musical was a delightful 20-minute journey into theatrical arts. This performance was a vibrant display of costumes, makeup, and song. Evelyn's standout performance as King Louie captivated the audience and set a high standard for future productions.
