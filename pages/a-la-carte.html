River Tech À La Carte

Flexible enrichment for homeschool families — choose 1 to 4 days per week.

River Tech offers flexible enrichment for homeschool families. Families choose how many days per week their student attends, and tuition scales accordingly. Our homeschool programs are for students ages 9-16, unless otherwise specified.

River Tech is a Christian school — all classes are taught with Christian commentary and a conservative moral framework. River Tech's teaching team holds college degrees across diverse fields and brings deep real-world experience to every classroom.

Location: 927 E. Polston Ave. Post Falls, ID. 83854
Fall Start: September 1st, 2026

Weekly Homeschool Schedule 2026-27

Four focused days of enrichment for ages 9-16. Choose the days that fit your homeschool family.

Day Subject Time
Monday Performing Arts 10:30 AM – 2:30 PM
Tuesday Science & Social Studies 10:30 AM – 2:30 PM
Wednesday Technology 10:30 AM – 2:30 PM
Thursday Life Skills & Electives 10:30 AM – 2:30 PM

Field Trips

Participants are also regularly invited to optional field trips, including hikes, farms, Silverwood, and other local experiences.

Who Thrives at River Tech?

Students ages 9+ who are curious, enjoy hands-on learning, and want to combine academics with the arts and technology. Whether your child loves to perform, build, create, or explore ideas, they'll find their place here.

Volunteering to Lead Teach a Class

If you have teaching experience and at least a bachelor's degree, or the equivalent expertise, you can apply to volunteer to teach a subject. As a volunteer, you won't be paid a salary — but in gratitude for your time, we do give priority to active volunteers during the scholarship review process.

Families who are unable to volunteer are still welcome to apply for tuition assistance — volunteering simply helps strengthen an application when funds are limited.
