Founder’s Office · Part-Time · 2026–27

AI-Fluent Executive Assistant

Take the weight off a founder’s hands — and help build a school for the future.

River Tech School of Performing Arts & Technology, in Post Falls, prepares grades 1–12 for an exponential future — arts and technology together, rooted in Christian conviction. The founder’s hours are the school’s scarcest resource. Your whole job is to give them back.

Also hiring: a full-time teacher and part-time teachers — and for the right person, there’s a teacher + EA combined role.

🧠

You run the agents

Direct the founder’s AI agents — Claude Cowork, Perplexity, and others — check their work, and turn raw output into finished product.

✍️

You write in his voice

Emails, grant applications, parent notes — written in his voice, so he can hand them to you and trust they’ll sound right.

📥

You own the inbox

Triage, respond, protect his attention. Catch the loose ends before they become problems.

🗓️

You keep it running

Calendar, invoices, forms, follow-ups. Handled quietly and well.

Who you are

  • AI-fluent and agent-positive, with documented, hands-on experience running AI agents — Claude Cowork, Perplexity Computer, or equivalent — to do real work.
  • A teacher at heart. You explain complex things simply, write with warmth and precision, and leave the people around you more capable than you found them.
  • A futurist. You probably already listen to Peter Diamandis on moonshots, watch what Elon Musk builds, and can’t stop asking how to do the thing better next time.
  • A Christian, and the faith at the heart of this school is your own.
  • Calm, discreet, fast, and precise — trusted with confidential work, and warm with the people you serve.
  • Efficient, agile, and stress-tolerant — you keep things moving and rise to high expectations.

Don’t have all of the above? Apply anyway. We’re looking for the right person more than the perfect résumé. If you have most of this — or a different mix of strengths that would let you do this job well — we’d genuinely love to hear from you.

The shape of it

Starts part-time. If it’s a fit, it can stay part-time or grow in hours with you. In person at the school in Post Falls, or hybrid. You’d work as an independent contractor.

For the right person, this role can also combine with teaching — see the Teacher + Executive Assistant combined role.

A college degree is welcome — the founder holds a BS and a Master’s — but it isn’t the gate. We care more about what you can do than what you’ve been certified to do.

How to apply

1

Apply below — about 20 minutes. Tell us how you’ve used AI agents and show us a little of your writing.

2

We review — the founder reads every application personally. If it’s a fit, we’ll reach out to talk.

3

We try it — the role starts part-time, so we both learn how we work together before it grows.
