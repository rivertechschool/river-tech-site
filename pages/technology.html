Technology

The Technology branch at River Tech is where innovation and wisdom meet. Coding, robotics, engineering, and digital design are not just tools for the workplace but instruments for building and stewarding God's world with excellence. Technology sharpens the mind, equips students with problem-solving skills, and prepares them to shape the future rather than be shaped by it.

At River Tech, we see technology as more than gadgets and screens — it is a calling to create, to innovate, and to bring order out of complexity. Every program written, every circuit connected, every prototype designed is an opportunity to reflect the brilliance of the Creator, who formed all things with precision and purpose.

"Whatever you do, work heartily, as for the Lord and not for men." — Colossians 3:23 ESV

Just as athletics trains the body and the arts shape the soul, technology strengthens the mind. Students learn perseverance in debugging, creativity in design, and discipline in engineering. Our vision is to raise up a generation that doesn't just consume technology but builds it, wields it responsibly, and glorifies God through innovation.

Join us as we cultivate inventors, builders, and problem-solvers who will shine with excellence, courage, and ingenuity in every arena of life.

CODING

At River Tech, we see coding as more than just a skill—it's a journey of discovery, creativity, and balance. From the start, we equip students with the tools to thrive in a world shaped by technology, while also ensuring they grow as well-rounded individuals grounded in Christian values and enriched by the arts. With initiatives like screen-free Wednesdays and structured coding sessions, we promote a healthy relationship with technology, blending innovation with balance and well-being.

Our progressive coding curriculum begins in second grade with Scratch Jr., nurturing logical thinking and problem-solving in a playful, approachable way. As students advance, they transition from Scratch into text-based programming languages like Python and C#, using platforms such as Unity to design games and apps. This step-by-step journey develops not only coding proficiency but also creativity and resilience.

Beyond coding, River Tech integrates performing arts, crafts, life skills, and faith, preparing students to meet the challenges of tomorrow as both skilled coders and thoughtful leaders—visionary creators shaped by wisdom, integrity, and imagination.

Coding Curriculum Progression

Scratch Jr. (2nd grade) → Scratch (block-based coding) → Python → C# with Unity (game & app development)

ROBOTICS

At River Tech School of Performing Arts & Technology, our robotics program takes students on a hands-on journey of discovery, beginning as early as second grade. Using the Vex Robotics curriculum, students learn the fundamentals of mechanical design by working with gears, shafts, standoffs, and other components to build projects like robotic arms, cars, and Ferris wheels. This foundation fosters both creativity and technical understanding, as students see how individual parts come together to form functioning machines.

From there, they bring their creations to life through coding, discovering the dynamic relationship between software and hardware. As their skills grow, students tackle Vex Go competitions that mirror real-world challenges, from ocean exploration to city infrastructure and even Mars missions. These projects emphasize teamwork, problem-solving, and innovation over competition alone. With a strong base established, students advance to Arduino, where they deepen their knowledge of robotics through open-source programming and more complex applications. Step by step, our program equips students with both the technical expertise and the critical thinking skills needed to lead in tomorrow's world of technology and engineering.

Robotics Curriculum Progression

Vex Robotics (foundational) → Arduino (advanced projects)

3D DESIGN

At River Tech School of Performing Arts & Technology, our 3D modeling program introduces students to the world of digital creation through Blender. From designing characters and environments to producing animations and 3D prints, students gain hands-on experience that unlocks the creative, technical, and spatial parts of the mind.

Each week, they learn new techniques—shaping objects, adding textures, lighting scenes, and animating movement—while steadily building confidence in their skills. Over time, students also learn to prepare and print their own models, connecting the digital to the physical.

Step by step, our program equips students with both the imagination and technical expertise needed to thrive in the ever-growing fields of digital media, animation, and design.

3D Design Tools

Blender (3D modeling, animation) + 3D Printing
