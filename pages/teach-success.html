What happens next

1. Personal review. The principal reads every application himself — your education, your experience, and especially what you can teach.

2. A conversation. If your subjects and availability fit the school’s needs, we’ll reach out to talk — usually within a few weeks.

Not a fit right now? Timing matters as much as talent — a subject we don’t offer this semester may be exactly what next year needs. Strong applications stay on file for future semesters and school years.

3. Teaching. New part-time teachers start in September, one day a week, with our full support.

Questions in the meantime? Email learn@rivertech.me.
