Release & Permissions

Please read before checking the box below.

Medical Emergency

I authorize River Tech School of Performing Arts & Technology and its staff to seek emergency medical care for my child if needed during River Tech Days. I will provide current emergency contact information and any critical health notes before the first day of attendance.

Liability Release

I understand that participation in River Tech Days involves normal physical activity (classes include drill, dance, and movement) and that River Tech School, The Heart, Faithful Five Inc., and their staff and volunteers are not liable for injuries arising from ordinary participation. I release and hold them harmless from claims related to my child’s participation, except in cases of gross negligence or willful misconduct.

Photo / Video Release

I grant permission for River Tech School and Faithful Five Inc. to photograph and record my child during River Tech Days and to use those images and recordings in print, digital, and social-media materials. If I do not wish my child to be photographed, I will email learn@rivertech.me before the first day of attendance.

Code of Conduct

I agree that my child will follow the basic expectations at River Tech: treat teachers and other students with respect, participate in good faith, and follow staff instructions. River Tech reserves the right to dismiss a student from the program without refund for conduct that endangers other children or disrupts the learning environment.

Christian Setting

River Tech is a Christian school. River Tech Days is open to families of any faith or none. Classes may include prayer, biblical references, and Christian framing. I understand and accept that my child may participate in these moments.
