Our Story

Our School Had a Fire. We're Already Back.

On March 15th, a fire damaged the building where River Tech meets at The River Church, destroying classrooms and school facilities. All students and teachers are safe. In true resurrection fashion, we secured a new home within days.

Where We Are Now: The Heart Church · 927 E Polston Ave, Post Falls, ID 83854. Classes resumed March 30th. We are deeply grateful to The Heart Church for welcoming us so quickly and generously.

River Tech is fully back and accepting students. We are not diminished — we are more motivated than ever to offer the best possible education for artistic and tech-savvy students.

Who We Are

Where Creativity and Innovation Converge

River Tech is a school of performing arts and technology based in Post Falls, Idaho. Our passionate teachers, comprehensive curriculum, and competency-based training model creates the perfect space for students to learn and grow.

At River Tech, creativity and innovation converge in a nurturing community. With class sizes around 15 students — roughly half that of public schools — our teachers are able to spend more time with each student. Grounded in enduring Christian values and a family-centered culture, our curriculum prepares students for life's realities, blending lessons in relationships, finances, and health with immersive technology and artistic expression.

New for 2025

Idaho Parental Choice Tax Credit

River Tech is thrilled to participate in the Idaho Parental Choice Tax Credit program, signed into law on February 27, 2025. This initiative offers up to $5,000 per child (or $7,500 for special needs students) to help cover tuition starting July 1, 2025.

Technology & STEM

In Today's World, S.T.E.M. is Important to Everyone

Tech (or S.T.E.A.M.) takes up the largest part of the curriculum at River Tech, with ample opportunity to learn everything from coding & robotics to 3D-modeling & videography.

Here at River Tech we believe everyone can benefit from a strong tech base. Even careers that are not normally associated with tech benefit from being confident and at home in a technological world.

  • Coding & Programming
  • Robotics
  • 3D Modeling & Animation
  • Game Development
  • Videography
  • Sound Engineering

Performing Arts

A Film, a Piece of Theatre, or a Piece of Music Can Change the World

Our students learn to play multiple instruments including drums, bass, ukulele, guitar, keyboard, vocals, acting, dance, choir, and band. We put on regular concerts and musical theater productions.

Our Arts Director, Dan Hegelund, is a professional vocal coach, musician, and choir director. His resume includes studio recordings, radio and TV broadcasts, and concerts in venues such as the Presidential Palace of Ukraine, Clash Of The Choirs, and Coca-Cola Holiday Caravan.

  • Theatre & Drama
  • Vocal Performance
  • Multiple Instruments
  • Dance
  • Choir & Band
  • Musical Theatre

Sports & Fitness

Preparing the Mind and Body

Our students learn mobility, sportsmanship, and an overcomer's mentality while playing sports and using professional workout equipment. We believe physical fitness is essential to a holistic education, teaching students to become masters over their bodies and train for longevity of life.

Our Athletics Director, Jerome Long, played in the NFL with the Kansas City Chiefs, Jacksonville Jaguars, and Dallas Cowboys. He is a certified math teacher with a B.A. in Mathematics. At River Tech, Jerome leads our math and sports programs, mentoring students in mind, body, and character.

  • Physical Education
  • Team Sports
  • Fitness Training
  • Sportsmanship
Our Foundation

The World May Shake, But Your Child Won't

In a world that sometimes seems to spiral out of control, River Tech can provide stability. Christian, family-centered, team-building, visionary, and leadership developing — at River Tech your child will discover purpose and meaning.

Our teachers possess a contagious joy, clarity, and hopefulness that will propel your child to take on life with renewed faith and optimism. Our curriculum is tailored to cultivating competency, mastery, and confidence in God, in themselves, and in their community.

Competency-Based Learning

Tailoring to Each Student's Needs

We accept students of grades K-12 and tailor each student's curriculum to their current academic level and needs. This keeps students from feeling held back or lagging behind, and instead fosters healthy learning environments.
